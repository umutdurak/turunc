About Us

Turunç is a Turkish Grunge band formed in Braunschweig, Germany by five friends originally from Ankara. Bringing backgrounds in computer science, aerospace engineering, and medicine, their sound merges precision with passion. Emre (lead guitar), Onur (vocals & rhythm guitar), Umut (bass guitar), Cem (keyboard), and Tuna (drums) breathe new life into Turkish rock classics from legendary bands like Duman, Mor ve Ötesi, Pilli Bebek, and Barış Manço.
