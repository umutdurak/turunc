About Us

Turunç is a Turkish Grunge band formed in Braunschweig, Germany by five guys originally from Ankara. Emre (lead guitar), Onur (vocals & rhythm guitar), Umut (bass guitar), Cem (keyboard), and Tuna (drums) breathe new life into Turkish rock classics from legendary bands like Duman, Mor ve Ötesi, Pilli Bebek, and Kurtalan Ekspress.
